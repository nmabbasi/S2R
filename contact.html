Frequently Asked Questions

How can I request a new tutorial topic?

We welcome suggestions for new tutorial topics! Please use the contact form above with the subject "Tutorial Request" and describe the topic you'd like us to cover. We prioritize topics based on community interest and relevance.

Do you offer personalized tutoring or consulting?

While our primary focus is on creating comprehensive tutorials for the community, we do offer limited consulting services for research projects. Please contact us with details about your specific needs.

Can I contribute content to Shell2R?

Yes! We're always looking for expert contributors to help expand our tutorial library. If you have expertise in bioinformatics and would like to contribute, please reach out to us using the contact form.

How do I report an error in a tutorial?

If you find an error or outdated information in any of our tutorials, please contact us immediately with the specific tutorial name and details about the issue. We take accuracy very seriously and will address any problems promptly.
