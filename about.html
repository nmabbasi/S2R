About Shell2R

Shell2R is an educational platform dedicated to teaching bioinformatics and computational biology through hands-on tutorials and practical examples.

Our Mission

We believe that learning bioinformatics should be accessible, practical, and engaging. Our tutorials are designed to take you from beginner to advanced practitioner, covering everything from basic command line skills to complex single-cell RNA-seq analysis.

What You'll Learn

  • Essential command line skills for bioinformatics
  • Package management with Conda and Mamba
  • Single-cell RNA-seq analysis techniques
  • Data visualization and interpretation
  • Best practices for reproducible research

About the Team

Shell2R is maintained by a team of bioinformatics researchers and educators passionate about making computational biology accessible to everyone.
