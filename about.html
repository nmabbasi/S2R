About Shell2R

A step-by-step platform for mastering bioinformatics and single-cell RNA-seq analysis — built to make complex computational biology concepts simple, practical, and actionable.

Our Purpose

Bioinformatics evolves rapidly. Staying current requires both theory and hands-on practice.

Shell2R bridges this gap with clear tutorials covering shell commands, HPC, and single-cell RNA-seq — empowering you to analyze real data confidently.
