About Shell2R

A step-by-step platform to learn Bioinformatics and Single-cell RNA-seq analysis with clarity and confidence.

Our Purpose

Bioinformatics is evolving fast, and keeping up with the latest tools can be overwhelming. Shell2R was created to bridge the gap between theory and practice through guided, hands-on tutorials.

Our goal: make complex topics like shell commands, HPC, and single-cell workflows simple, so you can apply them directly in your research.

Why Learn With Us?

  • ✔ Practical applications
  • ✔ Beginner & expert-friendly
  • ✔ Continuously updated content
